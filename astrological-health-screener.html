    Methodology & Limitations

    • Planetary positions use a compact analytical ephemeris (Schlyter's method) with the Lahiri ayanamsa for sidereal longitudes. Accuracy is within a few arc-minutes — sufficient for sign, nakshatra and KP sub-divisions.
    • Parashara analysis uses whole-sign houses; KP analysis uses Placidus cusps and cuspal sub-lords.
    • Interpretations are rule-based summaries of classical significations, not a substitute for an experienced astrologer or, above all, a medical professional.
